Behind the Build

Who We Are

Apex Raceline is a student-built electric go-kart team. Every part of the car — chassis, battery pack, motor controller, and driver software — is designed and built in-house by the crew you see above.

We compete in regional EV karting circuits and treat every race weekend as a live test of the systems we built in the shop the week before. Wins matter, but so does the data we bring home.

New members join a subsystem team from day one — no bench-warming. If you can solder, code, weld, or just love the smell of hot rubber on tarmac, there's a seat for you.

Vision & Mission

Driven by innovation, powered by passion

Vision:
To become a leading student-driven EV karting team that innovates sustainable mobility solutions and represents excellence in engineering, performance, and teamwork at national and international levels.

Mission:
To design and develop high-performance electric karts through continuous learning, innovation, and collaboration, while empowering students with practical engineering skills and promoting eco-friendly transportation technologies.
